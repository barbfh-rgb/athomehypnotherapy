GLP-1 tips
🥩Protein first. Eat protein before carbs. It preserves muscle while losing weight on GLP-1s.
🍽Small portions. GLP-1s slow gastric emptying — overeating causes nausea. Stop at the first sign of fullness.
💧Stay hydrated. Aim for 2–2.5L of water daily. Dehydration is a common side effect.
🚫Avoid trigger foods. Greasy, fried, or spicy foods worsen nausea. Carbonated drinks cause bloating.
🌿Fibre matters. Vegetables and legumes support digestion and help you feel full longer.
🐢Eat slowly. GLP-1s delay stomach emptying — eating too fast is a common cause of discomfort.
